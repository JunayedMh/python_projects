You are building a simple `grade reporting` tool for a school.
Write a program that takes a student's name and their numeric score (0-100),
then reports their letter grade and a short message.
**Rules:**
* 90 to 100 --> Grade A | `'Excellent` work!'
* 80 to 89 --> Grade B | `'Good job!'`
* 70 to 79 --> Grade C | `'You passed.'`
* 60 to 69 --> Grade D | `'Needs improvement.'`
* Below 60 --> Grade F | `'Unfortunately, you failed.'`
* If score is outside 0-100, print an `'error'` message.
* Output should greet the student by name.
